Course Description:
This course is an introduction to homological algebra. We will cover some basic notions in category theory, and give an introduction to derived categories and derived functors. If time permits, we will introduce some recent developments on hopfological algebra.
Textbooks:
We will be loosely following the classical textbook by Gelfand-Manin Methods of Homological Algebra. Our course will cover parts of Chapter II, III and IV of the textbook.
