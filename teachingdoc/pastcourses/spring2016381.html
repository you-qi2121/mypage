Course Description:
This course is aimed at giving a gentle introduction to noncommutative algebra. We aim to illustrate basic principles of abstract algebra via representation theory of some finite groups and finite-dimensional Lie algebras.
Prerequisites:
Basic undergraduate level abstract algebra, commutative algebra as in the first part of the course, some famliarity with finite groups, character theory will be assumed (see the first two chapters of [1]).
Referencess:
[1] J. P. Serre, Linear Representations of Finite Groups, GTM 42, Springer. The online version of the book can be downloaded on Yale campus through the link.

[2] W. Fulton and J. Harris, Representation Theory, A First Course, GTM 129, Springer. The online version of the book can be downloaded on Yale campus through the link.

[3] J. Humphreys, Introduction to Lie Algebras and Representation Theory, GTM 9, Springer, The online version of the book can be downloaded on Yale campus through the link.
